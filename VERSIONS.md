### v12.5.0 ‚Äî Keyless public-API wave: email-auth DNS, attention series, news mentions (2026-07-04)

Feature minor release. After the hosted-fetcher waves (Firecrawl v12.3, Tavily v12.4), this wave adopts the best **keyless public APIs** ‚Äî all live-tested 2026-07 ‚Äî as three new stdlib connectors plus seven recipe-level rows in CONNECTORS.md.

- **New connector `doh.py`** ‚Äî DNS-over-HTTPS (Google resolver, Cloudflare auto-fallback, both keyless): `auth <domain>` fetches the live SPF / DMARC (policy, rua, alignment tags) / BIMI / MX records and probes common DKIM selectors ‚Äî the email discipline's "paste a DNS export" step becomes a **Measured** record read for the SEND S1 pre-flight, for **any** ESP. **Facts only, no verdicts** (two record-level flags excepted: multiple SPF records = RFC 7208 permerror, and no `all`/`redirect` terminator); a record shows *setup*, not *passing mail* ‚Äî the DMARC RUA report stays required, and an unfound DKIM selector is NEEDS_INPUT, never a fail.
- **New connector `pageviews.py`** ‚Äî Wikimedia Pageviews (keyless, UA required): real per-article view series = a Measured public-attention trend for an entity or topic (pair with `kg.py reconcile` for the exact title). Attention is a demand *proxy*, not search volume.
- **New connector `gdelt.py`** ‚Äî GDELT DOC 2.0 (keyless): `artlist` news articles + `timelinevol` mention-volume trend ‚Äî the first keyless automation for `~~brand monitor` (previously manual Google-Alerts RSS only).
